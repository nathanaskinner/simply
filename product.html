Beyond Sausage

A modern miracle of meatiness crafted with plant based-ingredients.

Created from simple plant-based ingredients and applied in fresh ways. Peas, rice and fava beans provide the protein while trace amounts of beet lend a meaty color. Coconut oil ensures mouth-watering juiciness and it’s all wrapped inside a remarkable, 100% plant-based casing. The result is an uncompromisingly savory sausage, bursting with flavor, made directly from plants. Diversify your menu with three delicious flavors:

  • Original Brat

    A traditional Bratwurst flavor featuring a blend of nutmeg and savory spices. Pairs perfectly with spicy mustard and sauerkraut.

  • Sweet Italian

    Hints of fennel and classic Italian spices blend together for this time-honored flavor profile. A perfect protein to complement traditional pasta dishes.

  • Hot Italian

    Featuring notes of red pepper and garlic, Hot Italian adds a burst of spicy flavor when served on a bun or alongside sautéed peppers and onions.

Simple Facts

  • 16g Protein Per Serving
  • Vegan

Menu Items

Add Beyond or Beyond Sausage® to the item name description on your menu along with the item name.

  • Beyond Sausage® Korean-Style Dog: Beyond Brat topped with kimchi, carrot ribbons, green onions, sesame seeds and gochujang-flavored vegan mayonnaise*

  • Beyond Sausage® with Sweet Caponata: Serve Beyond Sausage® link in bun topped with Sweet Caponata consisting of celery, onion, eggplant, olives, pine nuts, capers, tomatoes paste, red pepper flakes, tomatoes, vinegar and sugar”

  • Beyond Tacos: Flour tortillas filled with grilled red onion, bell and poblano peppers, and crumbled Beyond Sausage® topped with green cabbage, cilantro and tomatillo-lime crema*

  • Beyond Sausage® Cajun Pasta: Penne pasta with sliced and seared Beyond Sausage®, mushrooms, tomatoes, onions and bell peppers in a Cajun-cream sauce topped with Parmesan cheese*

Sysco is the global leader in selling, marketing and distributing food products to restaurants, healthcare and educational facilities, lodging establishments and other customers who prepare meals away from home. Its family of products also includes equipment and supplies for the foodservice and hospitality industries. With over 65,000 associates, the company operates approximately 300 distribution facilities across the globe and serves more than 500,000 customer locations. For fiscal year 2017 that ended July 1, 2017, the company generated sales of more than $55 billion.
